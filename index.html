Animal Care Guide

Learn how to properly care for different animals

Dog Care

  • Provide fresh water and nutritious food daily
  • Take dogs for regular walks and exercise
  • Schedule regular vet checkups
  • Groom fur and trim nails as needed
  • Give attention, training, and love

Cat Care

  • Clean litter box daily
  • Provide fresh water and balanced food
  • Brush fur regularly
  • Provide scratching posts and toys
  • Visit the vet for vaccinations

Bird Care

  • Keep cage clean and spacious
  • Provide fresh water and bird-safe food
  • Allow time outside the cage for exercise
  • Provide toys for mental stimulation
  • Avoid drafts and extreme temperatures

Reptile Care

  • Maintain proper heat and lighting
  • Provide species-appropriate food
  • Keep enclosure clean
  • Monitor humidity levels
  • Handle gently and minimally

Fish Care

  • Keep the tank clean and properly filtered
