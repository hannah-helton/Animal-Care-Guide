Dog Care

  • Feed twice a day with dog food
  • Provide fresh water daily
  • Exercise every day
  • Visit the vet regularly

Cat Care

  • Feed cat food once or twice a day
  • Clean the litter box daily
  • Provide toys and scratching posts
  • Regular vet checkups

Bird Care

  • Clean cage weekly
  • Fresh food and water daily
  • Social interaction
  • Safe toys for stimulation
